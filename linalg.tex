\subsection{Determinant}

\subsubsection{$2 \times 2$ matrix}

For en $2 \times 2$ matrix
$$
\mtx{A} =
\begin{gmatrix}[p]
    a_{11} & a_{12} \\
    a_{21} & a_{22}
\end{gmatrix}
$$

defineres determinanten af $\mtx{A}$ som tallet
$$
\det{\mtx{A}} = a_{11}a_{22} - a_{12}a_{21}
$$
